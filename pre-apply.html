You warrant that every piece of information, document and statement provided is accurate, current and truthful. The Company is an Accountable Institution under FICA. You must produce certified KYC documentation. 7. Limitation of liability (CPA s49)

To the maximum extent permitted by law, the Company is not liable for any consequential, indirect, special or punitive loss. Nature of this fee. The amount of R 250.00 ("the Vetting Fee") is a fee for services rendered — specifically, the procurement of a credit and background verification report from a registered credit bureau (TPN Credit Bureau and/or TransUnion), together with the administrative costs of evaluating your application. The Vetting Fee is not a deposit, reservation fee, or consideration for any option or right to lease the property.

Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008 — disclosure. In terms of section 100 of the CPA, you are entitled to a clear disclosure of this fee before payment is made. By signing below, you confirm that this disclosure has been made to you in plain language and that you understand the nature, purpose, and non-refundability of the Vetting Fee prior to any payment being made.

Non-refundability. The Vetting Fee is strictly non-refundable in all circumstances once payment has been received, including but not limited to: (a) withdrawal of your application after payment; (b) your failure to qualify following vetting; (c) your decision not to proceed with a viewing following approval; (d) the property being let to another applicant. The non-refundability of this fee is not an unfair term under the CPA because the fee represents actual costs incurred by the landlord on your behalf, at your request, and the credit bureau report is generated specifically for your application.

No guarantee of viewing or lease. Payment of the Vetting Fee does not constitute an offer to lease, does not entitle you to a physical viewing, and does not create any obligation on the part of Napolitani Investments to conclude a lease agreement. A viewing invitation is extended solely at the landlord's discretion, subject to a satisfactory vetting outcome.

Payment instructions. Following receipt of this completed form, you will receive a payment reference and EFT banking details via email. Do not make payment until you have received these details in writing from info@napolifunds.co.za. Napolitani Investments accepts no responsibility for payments made to any other account.
